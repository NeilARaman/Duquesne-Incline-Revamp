Welcome to Our Shop

The Duquesne Incline Gift Shop, located in our Upper Station, offers a variety of unique souvenirs, historical memorabilia, and Pittsburgh-themed items. Every purchase supports the ongoing preservation and operation of this historic landmark, helping to ensure that future generations can continue to enjoy this piece of Pittsburgh's heritage.
